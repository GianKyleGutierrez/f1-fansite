Formula 1 2025 Teams

Mclaren

McLaren is a historic F1 team known for innovation and multiple championship wins. In 2025, they secured the Constructors' Championship.

Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing is a dominant modern team famous for aerodynamic excellence and multiple titles.

Mercedes

Mercedes dominated the turbo-hybrid era with Lewis Hamilton and strong technical performance.

Ferrari

Ferrari is the most historic F1 team, known for passionate fans and iconic cars.

Alpine

Alpine, formerly Renault, is a competitive team with a focus on performance and innovation.

Aston Martin

Aston Martin is a luxury brand making strides in F1 with strong investments and talent.

Williams

Williams is a legendary team with a rich history, striving to return to former glory.

KIck Sauber

Sauber combines heritage with modern F1 technology, aiming for competitive performance.

Haas

Haas is an American team focused on cost-effective engineering and steady progress in F1.

Racing Bulls

Racing Bulls is a new entrant in F1, aiming to make a mark with innovative strategies.
